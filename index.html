Brian Seonggeun Park

I am an undergraduate student studying Computer Science and Engineering at UCLA where I am a member of the Structures-Computer Interaction (SCI) Lab. My advisors are Professors M. Khalid Jawed and Jungseock Joo.

My research focus involves robotics, computer vision, and deep learning. More specifically, I have primarily worked in 3D Reconstruction from 2D images, as well as robot perception algorithms for detection of Deformable Linear Objects.
